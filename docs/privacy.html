🦆 FLOPPY DUCK

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 10, 2026

OVERVIEW

Floppy Duck is a casual flappy-bird-style game with multiplayer, a bot ladder, and cosmetic skins. We respect your privacy and collect only the minimum data needed to make the game work.

DATA WE COLLECT

  • Device Identifier — A random ID generated on first launch, used to save your progress and enable guest play. It is not linked to your real identity.
  • Apple ID (optional) — If you sign in with Apple, we receive a hashed user ID and optionally your display name. We never see your email unless you choose to share it.
  • Game Data — Scores, ELO rating, match history, skins owned, and bread (in-game currency). Stored in our backend to enable leaderboards and multiplayer.

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history or advertising identifiers

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Convex — Backend database for multiplayer, auth, and leaderboards. Data is stored securely in the US. Convex Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles in-app purchases. Payment info is processed entirely by Apple; we never see your payment details.

DATA RETENTION

Your game data is retained as long as you use the app. If you uninstall and never signed in with Apple, your guest data may be automatically removed after 12 months of inactivity.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Floppy Duck does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app does not contain ads or external tracking.

YOUR RIGHTS

You can delete your account directly in the app: go to Settings and tap "Delete Account" (available when signed in with Apple). This immediately removes your user profile, sessions, ratings, and matchmaking data. You can also contact us and we will remove your data within 30 days.
