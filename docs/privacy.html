Floppy Duck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Floppy Duck is an arcade game for iOS. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you play Floppy Duck, the following data may be collected:

  • User ID — A random device identifier and, if you sign in with Apple, an anonymized Apple user identifier. Linked to your gameplay profile for cloud sync.
  • Gameplay Content — Scores, wins, losses, match history, beaten bots, purchased items, and bread balance. Linked to your profile to persist progress across devices when signed in.
  • Product Interaction — Which screens you visit, which modes you play, and when you open the app. Used for analytics to improve the game. Not linked to your identity.
  • Crash Data — Crash reports and stack traces, collected via Sentry. Not linked to your identity.
  • Performance Data — App launch time, frame rates, and error rates. Not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Data

  • App Functionality — Cloud sync of your profile (scores, bread, purchases) via Convex; matchmaking and leaderboards; account authentication.
  • Analytics — Aggregated usage patterns to understand which features are used and identify areas for improvement (via PostHog).

3. Third-Party Services

Floppy Duck uses the following services which may process data on our behalf:

  • Convex (convex.dev) — Backend database for profiles, matchmaking, and cloud sync.
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — Crash reporting.
  • PostHog (posthog.com) — Product analytics.

None of these services receive your Apple ID, email, or payment information.

4. Sign in with Apple

Sign in with Apple is optional. When you use it, Apple provides an anonymized relay email (if you choose to hide your email) and a unique identifier. We do not receive your real email address unless you explicitly choose to share it.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Gameplay data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the in-app Settings menu (requires you to be signed in with Apple). Deleting the app from your device does not automatically delete your server-side data.

6. No Advertising or Tracking

Floppy Duck does not display advertisements, does not track you across other apps or websites, and does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA).

7. Children

Floppy Duck does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, to request data deletion, or to report an issue, visit the support page or contact us through the App Store.
