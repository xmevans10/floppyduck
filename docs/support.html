FAQ

How do I change my duck skin?
Go to the Closet from the home screen. Tap any owned skin to equip it. New skins can be purchased in the Shop or unlocked by beating bots on the ladder.
I bought a skin but it's not showing up!
Go to the Shop and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom. This will re-sync your purchases with Apple. If the issue persists, contact us below.
How does multiplayer work?
Tap Head to Head from the main menu. You can either match with a random player (Quick Play / Ranked) or create a private room and share the code with a friend.
What is bread?
Bread 🍞 is the free in-game currency. You earn it by playing games and maintaining daily streaks. Use it to buy normal-tier skins in the Shop.
How do I beat a bot?
In VS Bot mode, each bot has a target score. Reach that score in a single run to defeat the bot and unlock the next one. Beating a bot also unlocks their unique skin!
Can I delete my account?
Yes. Email us and we'll delete all your data within 30 days.
