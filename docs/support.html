Floppy Duck — Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap the screen to flap. Avoid the pipes. Collect bread. Beat bots on the bot ladder. Challenge other players in Head-to-Head mode.

What are the buttons on the home screen?

CLASSIC — Endless solo play. VS BOT — Ladder of 8 boss bots. HEAD TO HEAD — Multiplayer vs other players in Quick Play, Ranked, or Private Room.

Why do I need to sign in with Apple for some modes?

Ranked multiplayer and some bot ladder features require Sign in with Apple to prevent cheating and sync progress across devices. Classic and Quick Play are always free to play without signing in.

How do I change my username?

Go to Settings (gear icon on home screen) → tap the Player Name field, type your new name, and press Done. Names must be 2–16 characters and unique.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, scores, purchases, and match history from our servers.

I bought something but it didn't unlock

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If the item still doesn't appear, try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, contact support (see below).

Matchmaking isn't finding opponents

Multiplayer requires an internet connection. If you're on Wi-Fi, try switching to cellular (or vice versa). If you're in a Private Room, both players need to enter the same room code.

Contact

For bug reports, feature requests, purchase issues, or data deletion requests, please reach out through the App Store page for Floppy Duck or open an issue on GitHub.
