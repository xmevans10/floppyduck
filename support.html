Floppy Duck Support

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Use this page for help with Floppy Duck, including gameplay, purchases, account deletion, privacy requests, and bug reports.

Contact

For support, bug reports, purchase issues, or data deletion requests, open an issue at github.com/xmevans10/floppyduck/issues.

If you are contacting us about a purchase, include the item name and approximate purchase date. Do not include payment card details; purchases are processed by Apple.

Common Questions

How do I play?

Tap to flap, avoid pipes, collect bread, unlock cosmetics, climb the VS Bot ladder, and play multiplayer modes.

How do I restore purchases?

Open Settings or the Shop in the app and choose Restore Purchases. Restores use the Apple ID that made the original purchase.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings in the app and choose Delete Account. This removes the server-side profile data associated with your account. You can also contact support using the link above.

Why do some features require Apple or Game Center sign-in?

Linked identity helps sync progress, restore purchases, support leaderboards, and reduce multiplayer abuse.

Matchmaking is not finding an opponent

Check your internet connection and try again. Private rooms require both players to enter the same room code.
