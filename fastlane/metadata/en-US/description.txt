Floppy Duck is a retro arcade flier with boss ducks, real-time multiplayer, and collectible cosmetics.

Tap to flap, dodge pipes, and chase a new high score in Classic mode. Take on the VS Bot ladder to challenge 8 unique opponents, each with their own personality, skin, and style. When you are ready for live competition, jump into Head to Head for Quick Play, Ranked, or private rooms with friends.

Customize your duck with collectible skins, pipe skins, background themes, and battle banners. Earn bread by playing, unlock rewards, and equip your favorite look from the collection.

Track your best score, average score, win rate, rating, and streaks. Compare your skills on the global leaderboard and keep improving one flight at a time.

Floppy Duck is free to play with optional non-consumable cosmetic purchases.
