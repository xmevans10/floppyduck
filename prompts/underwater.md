# underwater Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art deep ocean water background. Seamless horizontal tile.
Very dark navy-blue at the top fading to medium teal-blue at the bottom. Subtle light rays streaming down from above — pale blue-white beams angling through the water. Tiny floating particles (plankton) — pale dots scattered throughout. No surface, no ground — just deep water.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. SKY/WATER ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art underwater landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Distant underwater rock formations and coral mounds in PALE hazy blue-teal, heavily obscured by water depth. Soft shapes, barely visible through the murky water. A few small fish silhouettes.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid sandy ocean floor, NOT white
- Formations reach about 45-50% of image height
- Above formations: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: very pale blue-teal, desaturated — everything looks hazy underwater
```

## foreground1
```
A pixel art underwater scene — CLOSE terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Vibrant coral reef — large colorful coral formations (orange, pink, purple), waving sea anemones, kelp fronds. A treasure chest half-buried in sand on one side. Bright tropical fish swimming nearby. RICH, SATURATED colors — this is the closest layer.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid sandy ocean floor, NOT white
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above tallest element: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: rich coral orange, pink, teal, purple — saturated and vivid
```
