# roughOcean Theme Prompt

## hero
```
16-bit pixel art hero background for a side-scrolling arcade flying game, rough storm ocean theme, wide 16:9 composition.
Dark teal-gray storm sky over a violent ocean, distant rain bands, rolling waves, whitecaps, sea spray, dark horizon, occasional far rock silhouettes. Highly detailed pixel art with strong contrast, stormy depth, cold blue-green palette, readable arcade composition.
Keep the central flight corridor clear and readable. Keep high waves and foam mostly in the lower third and far edges. No boats, no characters, no UI, no text, no pipes, no giant wave crossing the play path, no painterly/vector/3D style.
```

## runtime overlays
- Rain streaks, sea spray particles, lightning flash, low foam/wave trim, dark nautical obstacle skin.
