## background3
```

Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Distant LA skyline — downtown skyscrapers and palm tree silhouettes in HAZY warm purple-pink, softened by smoggy sunset atmosphere. The Hollywood sign barely visible on distant hills. Warm, dreamy haze.

```
