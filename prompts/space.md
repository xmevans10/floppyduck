# space Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art deep space background. Seamless horizontal tile.
Pure black at the top fading to very dark deep purple near the bottom. Dense star field — tiny white, pale blue, and faint yellow dots of various sizes. A distant colorful nebula cloud in one area (purple and blue wisps). The Milky Way as a faint band of light.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SPACE ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art space landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. An alien planet surface — distant craters and rocky ridges in PALE grey-purple, hazy with low atmospheric dust. Barren, otherworldly. A distant planet or moon visible on the horizon — large, pale sphere with surface detail.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid grey moon/planet ground, NOT white
- Terrain reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above terrain: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: pale grey, muted purple, desaturated — lunar/alien feel
```

## foreground1
```
A pixel art alien planet surface — CLOSE terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Close alien rock formations — jagged dark basalt with purple-tinted edges. Glowing alien crystals (cyan/teal) growing from cracks. A small space rover or planted flag on one side. Crater edge detail. DARK with luminous accents.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid dark moon rock, NOT white
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above tallest element: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: dark grey-black rock, glowing cyan/teal crystal accents, purple tint
```
