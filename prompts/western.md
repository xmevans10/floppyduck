## background3
```
A pixel art desert landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Flat sandy desert floor in warm tan-orange. Rising from it: Monument Valley style mesas and buttes — two tall formations on the sides, one wider mesa in center.
IMPORTANT COLOR NOTES:
- Mesas must be CLEARLY VISIBLE warm sandstone — NOT pale, NOT washed out, NOT white
- Use MEDIUM-SATURATION warm colors: golden-orange sandstone, warm sienna, dusty red-brown
- Think warm terracotta and golden sandstone, visible horizontal rock strata with warm brown/orange banding
- Desert floor: warm tan-orange with tiny dark green scrub dots
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid sandy ground, NOT white
- Mesas reach about 50-55% of image height
- Above mesas: pure white (#FFFFFF)
```
