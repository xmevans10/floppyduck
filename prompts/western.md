# western Theme Prompt

## hero
```
16-bit pixel art hero background for a side-scrolling arcade flying game, western desert theme, wide 16:9 composition.
Monument Valley-inspired desert at golden hour, distant mesas and buttes, warm terracotta sky, sandy flats, scrub plants, cracked earth and small rocks in the lower third. Detailed pixel art, strong shading, dusty depth, cohesive sienna/gold palette.
Keep the central flight corridor clean and readable. Keep cacti, rocks, and ruins low or at far edges. No characters, no UI, no text, no pipes, no giant cactus crossing the play path, no centered saloon/sign, no painterly/vector/3D style.
```
