# arctic Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art arctic sky. Seamless horizontal tile.
Pale icy blue at the top fading to soft white-lavender near the bottom. A low pale sun with a cool white glow. Faint aurora borealis wisps in soft green and purple. Thin wispy ice-crystal clouds.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SKY ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art arctic landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Distant snow-covered mountains and glaciers in VERY PALE icy blue-white — almost invisible against the sky. Soft, rounded snowy peaks. Everything is washed out and hazy.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid snow ground (pale blue-white), NOT pure white
- Mountains reach about 45-50% of image height
- Above mountains: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: very pale icy blue, lavender-white, barely visible
```

```
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above tallest element: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: white snow with cool blue shadows, pale cyan ice, dark pine green accent
```
