# arctic Theme Prompt

Frozen polar landscape at twilight: icy blue sky, pale lavender horizon, distant low glaciers, snowfields, frosty mist, subtle aurora glow, small sparkling ice pixels. Rich pixel shading, crisp silhouettes, restrained high-contrast accents, cohesive cold palette.
