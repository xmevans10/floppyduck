# lagoon Theme Prompt

## hero
```
16-bit pixel art hero background for a side-scrolling arcade flying game, tropical lagoon theme, wide 16:9 composition.
Bright turquoise lagoon, soft aqua sky, distant tiny islands, shallow reef colors, warm sand edge, small shells and driftwood in the lower third, palms kept to the far edges. Vibrant pixel art, crisp sunlight, sparkling water highlights, strong but clean depth.
Keep the central flight corridor clear and readable. Put tropical props low and away from the center. No characters, no UI, no text, no pipes, no large palm trunk through the play area, no boats blocking the center, no painterly/vector/3D style.
```

## runtime overlays
- Gentle cloud sprites, water sparkle particles, foam trim, palm/wood obstacle skin.
