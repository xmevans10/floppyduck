# neonCity Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art cyberpunk night sky. Seamless horizontal tile.
Dark purple-black at the top fading through deep magenta to neon pink-purple glow near the bottom (reflecting city lights from below). No stars — the light pollution washes them out. Subtle neon-tinged clouds.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SKY ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art cyberpunk cityscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Distant skyscraper silhouettes with tiny neon window lights in pink, cyan, and purple. Buildings are HAZY and DISTANT — dark purple-grey shapes with scattered glowing dots. A hazy neon glow at the base.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid dark ground, NOT white
- Buildings reach about 50-55% of image height
- Above buildings: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: dark purple-grey silhouettes, tiny neon pink/cyan/purple light accents
```

## foreground1
```
A pixel art cyberpunk street scene — CLOSE terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Dark building facades close-up — a neon sign on the left, a vending machine with cyan glow on the right. Wet pavement reflecting neon colors. Dark concrete with vibrant neon accents. SATURATED neon colors against dark surfaces.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid dark pavement, NOT white
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above tallest element: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: near-black concrete, bright neon pink, cyan, purple accents
```
