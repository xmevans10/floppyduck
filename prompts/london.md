# london Theme Prompt

## hero
```
16-bit pixel art hero background for a side-scrolling arcade flying game, foggy London theme, wide 16:9 composition.
Moody London skyline in soft rain: distant Parliament silhouette, clock tower shape without readable clock detail, chimney rooftops, wet cobblestone hints along the bottom, fog, puddle reflections, muted blue-gray palette with small warm window lights. Detailed pixel shading and atmospheric depth.
Keep the central flight corridor clean and readable. Keep lamp posts, signs, brick walls, and rooftops low or near the far edges. No characters, no UI, no text, no readable signage, no pipes, no giant tower blocking the center, no painterly/vector/3D style.
```
