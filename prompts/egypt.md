# egypt Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art Egyptian desert sky. Seamless horizontal tile.
Warm golden-yellow at the top fading to pale sandy cream near the bottom. A blazing hot sun — large, brilliant white-gold. Heat shimmer haze. Dry, ancient atmosphere. Almost no clouds — just faint heat distortion.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SKY ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art Egyptian landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Distant pyramids of Giza — three pyramid silhouettes in PALE hazy golden-tan, heavily washed out by heat haze and sand in the air. Flat desert stretching to the horizon. A distant obelisk shape.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid sandy ground, NOT white
- Pyramids reach about 45-50% of image height
- Above pyramids: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: very pale golden tan, sandy cream, heavily desaturated by heat haze
```
