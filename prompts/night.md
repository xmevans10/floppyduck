# night Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art night sky. Seamless horizontal tile.
Very dark navy-blue at the top fading to deep indigo near the bottom. Scattered twinkling stars — tiny white and pale blue dots at various sizes. A crescent moon glowing pale silver-white in the upper area. Faint wispy clouds in dark blue-grey.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SKY ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art night landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Dark rolling hill silhouettes in deep blue-grey, barely distinguishable from the night sky. Tiny pinpoints of warm light from a distant village. Moonlit haze softens everything.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid dark ground, NOT white
- Hills reach about 40-45% of image height
- Above hills: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: very dark blue-grey, near-black with subtle moonlit blue edges
```

## foreground1
```
A pixel art night landscape — CLOSE terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Dark terrain with a gnarled tree silhouette on the left, rocks and tall grass on the right. Fireflies (tiny warm dots) scattered about. Everything is VERY DARK — near-silhouette with subtle cool moonlit edges.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid dark ground, NOT white
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height
- Above tallest element: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: near-black, very dark blue-green, tiny warm firefly accents
```
