# sunset Theme Prompts

## background1
```
A pixel art sunset sky. Seamless horizontal tile.
Deep purple at the top transitioning through rich orange-red to golden-yellow at the bottom. A large setting sun — half visible near the bottom edge, brilliant orange-gold with warm rays. Wispy clouds catching the sunset in pink and gold.
Smooth gradient. Fully opaque. No terrain — SKY ONLY.
```

## background3
```
A pixel art sunset landscape — DISTANT terrain. Seamless horizontal tile. White background above terrain.
Content fills from THE VERY BOTTOM EDGE upward. Gentle rolling hill silhouettes in warm dusky orange-brown, heavily hazed by sunset atmosphere. VERY SOFT and PALE — almost silhouette shapes against the golden sky.
- BOTTOM ROW must be solid ground color, NOT white
- Hills reach about 40-45% of image height
- Above hills: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Colors: muted warm orange, dusty rose, soft brown
```

```
- Tallest element reaches about 40-45% of image height

```
