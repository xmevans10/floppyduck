Floppy Duck Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Floppy Duck is an iOS arcade game. This policy explains what data is collected, why it is used, and how to request deletion.

Data We Collect

  • User identifiers: a device identifier generated by the app, session tokens, and, when you sign in, Apple or Game Center identifiers and display names. These are used for account functionality, cloud sync, leaderboards, and multiplayer.
  • Gameplay data: scores, wins, losses, ELO, match results, Battle Royale entries, beaten bots, bread balance, cosmetics, selected skins, and similar progress data.
  • Purchase state: purchased and restored cosmetic items. Payment details are handled by Apple and are not received by Floppy Duck.
  • Product interaction data: app opens, mode selection, shop views, gameplay events, and similar analytics events used to improve the app.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports, performance information, error logs, device/app context, and server diagnostic events used to fix bugs and reliability issues.

How Data Is Used

  • To operate the game, save progress, restore purchases, support account deletion, and provide multiplayer, friends, and leaderboard features.
  • To prevent abuse, debug matchmaking, diagnose crashes, and improve performance.
  • To understand aggregate app usage and improve gameplay and user experience.

Third-Party Services

Floppy Duck uses service providers to operate the app:

  • Apple: Sign in with Apple, Game Center, App Store purchases, and purchase restoration.
  • Convex: backend database, authentication sessions, cloud sync, leaderboards, matchmaking, and diagnostics.
  • Sentry: crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • PostHog: product analytics.

No Advertising Tracking

Floppy Duck does not show third-party ads, does not sell personal data, does not use the advertising identifier for tracking, and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay and account data is kept while your account exists or as needed to operate the app. You can delete your account from the in-app Settings screen where available. You can also request deletion through the support page.

Children

Floppy Duck is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact support so it can be reviewed and deleted if appropriate.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, visit the support page.
